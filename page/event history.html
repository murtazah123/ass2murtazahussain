This is the Event history page

Join us for our upcoming events—come with friends or come alone; you’ll always find a warm and friendly group to welcome you.

Easter Workshop (6th April) Step into the world of Slovak Easter traditions at our Easter Workshop, where culture, creativity, and community come together for a fun and welcoming afternoon. This hands-on event will offer something for everyone—families, individuals, and anyone who enjoys arts, crafts, and festive traditions. Join us in decorating eggs using traditional Slovak techniques, creating beautiful Easter crafts, and learning about the unique customs that make Slovak Easter so special. But this event is more than just a workshop—it's also a social gathering where you can meet new people, enjoy great company, and experience a warm community atmosphere. Whether you're coming with friends, family, or on your own, you’ll find a friendly, relaxed environment where connections happen naturally over delicious Slovak food and drinks. To make the afternoon even more special, we will have music playing throughout the event and, if all goes to plan, we hope to welcome a special guest.
